---

> 
> Welcome to **Somnia Reactor**. We’ve built more than a dashboard; we’ve built an **Autonomous DeFi Intelligence Engine**.

## 🕒 Phase 2: The Reactor Core (0:45 - 1:30)

### 💡 Pro-Tips for Recording:
1. **Speed**: Don't linger on pages. The "Reactivity" theme works best with snappy transitions.
2. **Audio**: Use a professional mic or clear voiceover to contrast the high-tech visual aesthetic.
3. **Red Dot**: Ensure the agent's "Red Dot" cursor (or your own) is highlighting the specific UI elements mentioned (e.g., the Feed, the Guard status).
